About Us

Incorporated in 2022, E POWERON FUTURE SOLUTION PRIVATE LIMITED is a dynamic company based in Kerala, specializing in providing comprehensive solutions in Tower, Fiber, Power, and Space. Our leadership comprises Mr. Ibrahim Muhammad and Mrs. Fathima, who bring over 7 years of experience in the Telecom Industry with major Telecom OEM and Infra, Fiber network companies.

We pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards, while maintaining a focus on continuous improvement. Our services include Fiber to the Home for residential/apartment projects, shopping malls, and commercial buildings, turn-key solutions for entire campuses including design, supply, and installation, and outdoor fiber connectivity for operators and infra providers.

We are committed to developing and delivering excellent business solutions aimed at increasing end-user revenue, enhancing satisfaction, and providing a real competitive edge for our customers. Our company endeavors to provide qualitative service with qualified personnel and well-equipped tools, maintaining a strong focus on quality of service and consistently exceeding clients' expectations.

Our policies prioritize no child labor, no forced employment, non-discrimination, adequate training, and a performance-based rewarding system. We focus on employee health and safety, ensuring that safety remains a priority in our business.

Our infrastructure includes our own ROW team across Kerala, a dedicated manpower team of 10 employees, machinery with operators for HDD machines, cable blowing machines, and backhoe loaders.
